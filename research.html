Research

SeeBeyondBorders prides itself on using evidence-based programmes to deliver quality teaching and learning to Cambodia children. Here in Ireland our organisation has developed strong links with various educational institutions including schools, universities and teaching organisations. In the year ahead we hope to continue to strengthen our research links. We will host an educational networking webinar on July 7th 2021 thanks to receiving a networking grant from DCU.

We will continue to seek research opportunities to support the work of SeeBeyondBorders on the ground in Cambodia. We will explore the feasibility of collaborating with a research institution in Ireland for the COALESCE Irish Research Council Funding in January 2022.
