Partnership

While strong relationships and networks have existed for several years between Ireland, 2020 has provided new barriers to further strengthen these bonds. The start of the year saw a very positive visit from SBB staff and representatives from the Minister of Education to Ireland, this gave us a fantastic opportunity to really engage our Irish network and we had an incredibly successful trip which reached out to many corners of this network.
While COVID-19 posed barriers to our work it also meant we needed to call upon our Irish supporters to fund a solidarity fund for staff and their families in Cambodia who had been badly impacted by the epidemic. This was met with an incredibly generous response and further reinforces our view of being able to rely on this network to step up when needed to support our mission.
